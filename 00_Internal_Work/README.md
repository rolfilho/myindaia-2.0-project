## The one-paragraph orientation

Indaiá has hired us to answer *what to build, how, for how much, and in how long* — not to build
it. The contractual deliverables are in
[Anexo I](../00_Scope/Anexo_I_Escopo_e_Insumos_Discovery.md) §1.1 and are the definition of done.
Three planning artifacts already exist and disagree with each other in places (the CEO Vision, the
internal export build, and our own v0 migration plan); reconciling them into **one** plan is
deliverable (a). One decision — OCR — is tested empirically. Everything else is resolved by
analysis and recorded with its trade-offs.
